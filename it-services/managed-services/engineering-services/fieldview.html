FieldView: our field engineer management software

Ultra-responsive IT support across the world

Our proprietary software, FieldView, provides fully automated, end-to-end engineer scheduling and management.

It is a centralised control system for every ticket and every engineer across the globe and gives you real-time visibility of how our team is looking after your business.

Fieldview enables ultra-responsive IT support, reduced service desk time and vastly improved management data in real-time and at a reduced cost. It empowers our global field force of 1,000+ engineers to resolve incidents faster while keeping you up to speed with every development.

How does FieldView work?

Every incident is logged by a customer and then automatically fed through to FieldView, detailing the user, location, device or system and reported fault. 

The ticket gets authorised and automatically assigned to an engineer, based on their availability, skillset and location. The engineer accesses the full case history and remote diagnostics via a mobile-app. 

Customers can track and monitor everything in real-time on their bespoke portal, including details of the engineer assigned, status, SLAs and cost. 

FAQs

Can I use FieldView with an external service management platform?

Yes, FieldView connectivity with service management platforms such as Remedy and ServiceNow can be achieved through an EDI or API connections.

Will our systems and data be secure when connected to FieldView?

Yes, our systems are fully protected and aligned with ISO 27001 best practices. We also meet all European Data Security and GDPR standards.

How can I be sure that I will get a top quality engineer on-site?

All our engineers are carefully selected, pre-vetted and fully trained to your required standards before they start working for you.

How often can I review service performance?

You will have access to your own customisable portal where you can monitor service performance in real-time, including checking engineers are on-site, viewing calls that have breached SLAs and customer satisfaction statistics.

Have you heard about TrainView?

TrainView is our proprietary global e-learning platform, delivering bespoke online training programmes, assessments and onboarding. It ensures engineers are always suitably trained, compliant and accredited before arriving on site to work for your organisation
