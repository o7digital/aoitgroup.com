Global reach, but a personal touch

We are big enough to master the IT challenges of multi-national organisations, but small enough to understand the importance of honest one-to-one communication. The service we deliver builds lasting customer relationships, making us the perfect choice for start-ups through to large outsourcing partners.

Heritage you can trust

Originally an IBM company in the 1960s, the A&O IT Group has been conquering the needs of our customers for more than 50 years. We have a proven track record of delivering game-changing solutions and protecting the reputations of all those we work with and for.

Champions of innovation & automation

The ongoing improvement of our proprietary technology helps us generate innovative solutions, maximum efficiencies and reduced costs for all our customers. We are determined that the service we deliver for you tomorrow will be better than the service we deliver today.

Class-leading cyber security solutions

Our cyber security experts have protected organisations across many industries including banking, insurance, recruitment, retail and utilities, supporting a wide range of businesses from SMEs to multinational organisations. No customer is too small (or too big).

Truly worldwide solutions

What makes us global is not just working in over 100 countries, it’s the consistent processes and levels of service we provide. Whatever your time zone or language, our expert engineers are ready when you need them. Wherever you happen to be in the world, you’ll always know exactly where you are with A&O IT Group.
