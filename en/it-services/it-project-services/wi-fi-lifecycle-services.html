Wi-Fi Lifecycle Services

In today's fast-paced business landscape, end-user experience is paramount for IT leaders. With the exponential shift from wired connections to Wi-Fi, having a consistent, stable, and fast Wi-Fi network is no longer a luxury—it's a necessity. Video calls now constitute a large proportion of a typical workday, making network stability crucial.

A&O IT Group has developed world-class solutions to support the entire lifecycle of a Wi-Fi network, offering consultation, strategic development and deployment to deliver significant improvements. Leveraging AI technology, we reduce time planning, on-site, and executing, ensuring optimal performance in any global environment.

Wi-Fi solutions tailored for you

Struggling with Wi-Fi challenges? Whether it's connectivity issues, dead zones, or network security, we understand the unique needs, locations, and complexities you face. At A&O IT Group, we address these challenges head-on with tailored solutions designed specifically for you.

Utilising our proprietary FieldView platform and a suite of industry-leading tools, we blend human expertise with advanced technologies to deliver unparalleled value, ensuring your network is reliable and robust.

  • FieldView - our world class resource planning system
  • Ekahau AI - our primary global surveying tool set
  • AirMagnet - our reliable classic tool for simple environments
  • NetSpot - our small site solution for rapid surveying
  • JVSG - our CCTV planning tool

We can assist your industry
 

Office Wi-Fi solutions

Enterprise and office locations require high density connectivity to a fixed user base, with high bandwidth being the priority for users.

Accounting for meeting rooms, BYOD requirements, from small sub office locations to major campus sites, we can deliver the correct enterprise office Wi-Fi solution for your needs.

Retail Wi-Fi solutions

Retail environments are becoming more and more complex and have much higher requirements due to digitisation. Supporting POS terminals, cameras, video walls, audio systems and loss-prevention sensors requires more and more Wi-Fi at varying bandwidth needs. 

With our ability to understand flagship to pop-up location requirements, we can help you navigate these unique requirements.

Manufacturing Wi-Fi solutions

Wi-Fi performance in complex manufacturing sites faces unique challenges, including high ceilings and outdoor coverage needs.

The convergence of IT and OT in Industry 4.0 demands exceptional network performance. We excel in these environments, leveraging our expertise and ISO 45001 accreditation to ensure safe, secure, and high-performing Wi-Fi solutions.

Education Wi-Fi solutions

With a high number of users and increasing security requirement, educational establishments are complex environments, especially given the restriction on availability to work for holiday periods.

From secondary to higher education, from single buildings to multi location campus we’ve got the expertise to work within the restrictions and safeguarding and complex legacy environments to bring your Wi-Fi requirements up to speed.

Warehouse Wi-Fi solutions

Warehouse environments often face connectivity issues like dead zones and interference, disrupting operations and reducing productivity. A&O IT Group's Warehouse Wi-Fi Solutions tackle these problems through directional and sectional antennas, detailed site surveys and optimised network designs.

We install robust access points and minimise interference, ensuring dependable connectivity for all devices. This approach supports automation, boosts productivity, and ensures smooth, efficient warehouse operations.

 

Full Wi-Fi lifecycle services

Wi-Fi planning

Full Wi-Fi service for understanding what you have today and where you stand. From desk-based audits to onsite APoS auditing - we can provide the right guidance for you.

Wi-Fi survey and design

If you already understand your environment and have a plan to improve, we can do the onsite and offsite audits in order to validate, plan and prepare for your migration.

Wi-Fi warehousing and hardware delivery services

Complex global Wi-Fi deployments require logistical backup and know-how to ensure that the right equipment arrives just in time for your migration. We have the experience to transform your delivery at global scale.

Wi-Fi deployment services

No matter the size and scale of the environment, we have the teams and the know-how to provide a seamless deployment experience. Vendor-agnostic we can work in a wide array of location types.

Wi-Fi IMAC / break-fix

Providing onsite services throughout the lifecycle of your estate we can provide hard changes to the environment with post-deployment survey validation as well as providing a full break-fix solution no matter where you need it.

Wi-Fi disposal

As you migrate and upgrade through your lifecycle, knowing where your assets end up is critical to ensure environmental and ethical best practice is followed. We provide a fully secured process to assist in your compliance requirements.
