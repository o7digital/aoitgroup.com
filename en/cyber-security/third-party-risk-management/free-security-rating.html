Your free security rating report

What is the security rating report?

This report provides a comprehensive overview of your organisation's current security risk rating, for free.

RiskRecon's third-party security risk monitoring technology is used to assess cyber risk and generate a report of vulnerabilities.

How can you use this Security Rating Report?

Knowing your security rating is the first step to improving your overall cybersecurity risk. RiskRecon's focus on external discovery can help you identify how attackers may target your organisation's assets, which could lead to a critical data loss event.

By understanding your security rating, you can take proactive steps to protect yourself against potential threats.

 

What can you expect from this Security Rating Report?

- Access a snapshot of your current security rating for a limited period of time to understand what the findings mean for your risk score across multiple security domains.

- Compare your organisation's cybersecurity performance to competitors in your industry.

- Use the score to take corrective action with risk-prioritised data based on issue severity.

- Track your security roadmap and maturity level as well as reporting on compliance and regulations.
