Bespoke solutions for the public sector

Providing everything from digital video systems to cyber security services, we deliver effective solutions before issues become problems.

A&O IT Group have been solving IT challenges in the public sector for many years. Our accomplished team have a proud record of developing bespoke solutions to meet the demands of a wide range of public sector departments. Our reputation for excellence comes from both our technical expertise and our determination to work tirelessly and efficiently to solve any kind of problem.

Emergency Services

A&O IT Group offers a range of tailored solutions to drive efficiency and performance for the Emergency Services, fully understanding the specific challenges encountered every day.

All our solutions are fully compliant and designed to industry standards, meeting data legislation and privacy requirements. We have a strong track record for meeting the full range of IT needs of the Blue Light Services, including safeguarding solutions, welfare solutions and lone worker protection.
