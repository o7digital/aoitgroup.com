How our global IT outsourcing services can benefit you

 

Consistent global service

Consistent, high quality end user support wherever you are and whatever your time zone. Our UK headquarters is supported by offices in France, Germany, Nordics, UAE, Italy, USA and India.

Industry-leading technology 

Our proprietary software, FieldView, provides fully automated, end-to-end engineer scheduling and management. It is a centralised control system for every ticket and every engineer across the globe and gives your clients real-time visibility of how our team is looking after their business.
 

Our proprietary e-learning platform

TrainView is our proprietary global e-learning platform, delivering bespoke online training programmes, assessments and onboarding. It ensures engineers are always suitably trained, compliant and accredited before arriving on site to work for your clients.

Fully managed service 

We provide asset management expertise to keep employees fully active at work and to manage lifecycle requirements for IT hardware assets. This includes taking responsibility for all ticket delivery requirements, service desk, warehousing, logistics and field service engineers.  

Unrivalled experience

We support a wide range of organisations across all verticals with intelligent software solutions that drive automation and proactive network monitoring. We are the partner of choice for many global organisations including cloud and infrastructure outsourcers, system integrators and hardware manufacturers.

HR Expertise

Skilled in TUPE and ARD, our HR team understands how employment regulations vary across the world. We will happily take responsibility for employee consultation, vetting, on-boarding, training, payroll services and on-going management.
